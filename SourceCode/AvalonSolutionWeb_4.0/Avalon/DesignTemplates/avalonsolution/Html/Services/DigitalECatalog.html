Avalon Solution’s Print To Digital ECatalog Service

Avalon Solution offers Print To Digital ECatalog Service that will enable you to showcase your printed catalogs and flyers Online - 24/7. An ECatalog is an Electronic Online Version of your paper catalog or brochure. Our turnkey service will digitize your printed catalogs and flyers and place them online for easy viewing from your website or linked from your promotional email marketing at a fraction of the cost of mail distribution.

Why Use our Print to Digital ECatalog Service?

If you want to reach today's consumer that may not be reading the local newspaper and spends much of their time browsing the internet and researching products online before buying - it is essential to have a digital version of your printed catalogs and brochures available Online 24/7. In addition, if you want to have your printed catalog available on demand to reach a wider audience, as well as lower your distribution costs and increase sales, it is critical that you include in your marketing arsenal - Avalon Solution's Print To Online Digital ECatalog Service. Just give us your digital files and we will convert your Printed Catalogs, Flyers and Brochures into professional looking Digital ECatalogs with the ability to flip pages, zoom-in, along with print and save options.

Your Online Digital ECatalog will be the same as your Printed Catalog along with your Logo, Customized Brands and Prices. In addition we will create, host and place your Digital ECatalogs on your own Customized URL which can be linked from your website or promotional emails. Unlike the increasing costs incurred by distributing additional copies of your printed catalogs via newspaper or direct mail, the cost of a Digital ECatalog does not rise with increasing views. In addition, to maximize the return on your marketing - you can utilize this service in combination with Avalon Solution's Email Marketing Campaign Services.
