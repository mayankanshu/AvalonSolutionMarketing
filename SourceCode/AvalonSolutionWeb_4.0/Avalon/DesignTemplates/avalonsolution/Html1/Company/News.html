Avalon Solution integrates Amazon Payments for a Seamless Buying Experience

APRIL 01, 2014

View article online

New York, NY — Avalon Solution has recently integrated Amazon Payments option in its Ecommerce Solutions. Now retailers can add Pay with Amazon as a Payment Method on their Websites to make their checkout process simpler and seamless.

Millions of Amazon buyers can login and pay on your website or mobile site with the information already stored in their Amazon account. It's fast, easy and secure and helps you add new customers, increase sales, and reduce fraud costs.

This allows online retailers to create long-term relationships with more customers. Pay with Amazon helps replace guest checkouts with recognized customers, leading to improved services which include: managing and tracking orders, purchase history detail, special discounts, instant access to shipping addresses and payment methods.

With Amazon Payments, website visitors can avoid account signup and use their Amazon Account to buy products available on your website. When a Website Visitor clicks on Amazon Payment Method, a Login Popup Window appears and once they enter their Amazon.com Login, they can use their Amazon Account Information to complete purchases.

Website visitors want the advantage of creating an account to save purchase preferences when shopping online, but the majority are bothered by the hassle of registering on a website. Increasingly, they choose to shop on sites that offer seamless online payment options to take advantage of stored information. That is why Amazon has introduced this payment solution where buyers can pay on any participating site using their Amazon credentials.

When your Website Visitors select Pay with Amazon option, they can use the information stored in their Amazon account to pay conveniently. Pay with Amazon offers Safe and Seamless Online Shopping with many benefits:

  • Amazon Payments maintains your customer’s privacy and does not use any purchase history for their own Amazon.com use
  • Amazon has over 215 million active customer accounts
  • Uses the information from Amazon.com account to complete purchases
  • When they login with Amazon, you get the customer's info so you can create their online account
  • No need to re-enter credit card numbers or shipping addresses
  • Offers the convenience and familiarity of Amazon's checkout experience
  • Transactions are secure because financial information is not shared or stored with merchants
  • Low transaction costs compared with other payment option
  • Amazon payments easily integrates with your Mobile ready website
  • The same buyer protection plan on Amazon.com is available when your customers checkout using Amazon Payments
