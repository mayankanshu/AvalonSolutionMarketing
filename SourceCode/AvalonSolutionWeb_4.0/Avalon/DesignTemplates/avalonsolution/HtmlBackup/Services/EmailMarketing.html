Why Email Marketing?

According to surveys, customers prefer to hear from you via Email than any other method. According to research conducted by the Direct Marketing Association, email marketing generated an ROI of $44.25 for every dollar spent in 2013.

You have to constantly communicate with your customers to build and maintain relationships, increase sales and enhance customer loyalty. Email has added a new dimension to businesses as more and more consumers use emails and shop online. It has virtually empowered businesses, small and large. There are many reasons, why your business should utilize Email Marketing:

  • Email Marketing is affordable, direct, actionable and highly effective.
  • When you run an Email Campaign, you spend less time, money and resources than with any other traditional marketing channels such as direct mail, print advertising and TV Commercials.
  • It is a highly effective way to increase sales, drive website or store traffic and develop loyalty.
  • You can easily segment your lists using a variety of criteria or interest groups so that your promotions go to the individuals most likely to respond to your offer.
  • With Email Marketing, you can communicate more quickly and generate an immediate response.
