Your website comes equipped with a very sophisticated and easy to use Content Management Admin Section that will allow you to customize and manage most of the things on your website - right from your company info to the payment gateway availability. The objective of Admin Section is to help you in managing your website in correct look and design. With the help of this Admin Section, you will be able update your company information, add new products, manage pricing and customer information, and much more according to your needs.

Create and manage your own customized web pages, marketing campaigns and promotions under relevant web pages. Use our easy-to-use WYSIWYG (What-You-See-Is-What-You-Get) editor and publish all the latest news, events and information regularly on your website. To use this Content Management System, you don’t need to be a computer expert. You do not need to install any software on your computer to run your Website’s Admin Section. It loads in your web browser and allow you to make required changes in your Website.

Content Management Features

Slider Management

Manage Home Page Sliders by showcasing beautiful pictures of your store, the brands you carry, happy customer moments, upcoming special events, or any other occasion.

Menu Management

Now create tabs/menus as per your business requirements for ex. Jewelry or Watch Brands, Apply for Financing, We Buy Gold or Custom Jewelry Making.

Wish List & Shopping Bag Analysis

Search and lookup customers with active wish list or shopping bag along with their info so you can email or call them right away to offer a special deal.

Customers Management

Search, manage and add new customers to your website. You can also export customer info in an excel file for easy upload onto your emailing software.

Custom Page Management

Create and add new custom pages to your website using an easy to use Text Editor or edit default pages like About Us, Testimonials, FAQ’s, Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, etc.

Coupon Management

Add Digital Coupons to your website offering special discount to increase customer sign ups. Customers get a print copy of coupon to redeem at your Store.

Company/Store Info

Edit your company’s contact information, your business days & hours, and other vital information about your company. Also add multiple store locations to your website.

Product & Pricing

Manage and update prices of the products displayed on your website. You can define markup or discount on any individual product or a group of products.

Secure Shopping Cart

Control the availability of different Payment Gateways on your website to enable your customers to purchase your products online using safe and secure shopping cart and pay by methods of their choice such as checks, bank transfers, credit card, Pay with Amazon, PayPal and Order by Phone.

Promotional Email marketing

Admin Section also provides you broad web-based marketing and promotional facilities. You will be able to manage target based email marketing campaigns for your customers and registered users.

Product Upload

Upload your best selling jewelry, watches and gift products along with images and full details on your website. Your uploaded products will be seamlessly integrated with your existing jewelry products all in the jewelry categories and designer collections of your choice.

Category Management

Category Management is integrated with the Product Upload section which enables you to easily create and manage new product categories on your website and assign your uploaded products under these categories.

Digital Coupon & Email Subscriptions

Create and manage promotional and discount Coupons on your website. You can specify the date range for coupons to appear on your website. When your website visitors subscribed for a coupon by providing their name and email id, they get a discount coupon in their Email Inbox. You can use this valuable customer database for future promotional purposes.

Search Engine Optimization

Optimize your Website to enable Search Engines to read and index your website contents properly. You can dynamically optimize SEO text and keywords of your website’s Intro Page, Home Page and Custom Pages on your store’s key features, geographic location, seasonality etc.

WYSIWYG Text Editor & Image Uploader

Select from a variety of formatting tools to format your text and edit and upload your own custom images.

Emails

Write up or edit your own contents to customize the various automatically generated emails on your website.
