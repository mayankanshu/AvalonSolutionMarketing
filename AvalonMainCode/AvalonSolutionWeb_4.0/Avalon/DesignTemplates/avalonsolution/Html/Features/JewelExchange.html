JewelExchange® is an innovative Digital Marketplace built specifically for the Jewelry Industry where Retail Jewelers connect with their Brands & Suppliers to increase their market share and grow their brand.

It is vital for you as a Retailer to have quality content available on your website - where customers and prospects can view your best-selling products and collections online 24/7. This is where JewelExchange comes in. JewelExchange creates a bridge between you - the Jewelry Retailer and your Suppliers; it allows you to get your all supplier’s products, style images and POS marketing materials all in one place.

JewelExchange Features:

  • Easy to use online web-based application
  • Quick access to manage all of your suppliers’ products and images in one place
  • Publish your supplier’s product to your website in just seconds
  • Suppliers take care of product and image updates
  • Invite your existing suppliers and search prospective ones
  • Showcase and sell suppliers’ entire product line or selected collections
  • Display high-quality style images directly from your supplier
  • Download suppliers’ POS marketing material

Support

  • Successfully supporting over 500 Independent Retail Jewelers Storefronts
  • Dedicated 24/7 Support Team
  • Assistance in Uploading your Inventory
  • Highly Customizable Websites available

Why YOU should be a part of JewelExchange

  • Grow your business and build your brand - With most customers searching online for bridal and fashion jewelry before making their purchase decision it is critical to showcase your best sellers on your website 24/7.
  • You are in control - No one knows your business better than you do, and no one knows what products or brands you want your customers to see, or how you want them to look. You have direct control over your Suppliers’ content on your website.
  • Save your time - You don't have to worry about uploading products and style images to your website – since your Suppliers will provide their product feed directly to your website - all with up-to-date info.
  • Don’t lose sales - By allowing your online visitors to go outside your website and be redirected to suppliers Store Locator to shop your competitors.
  • Maximize profits - Give your own retail price markups and maximize your profits.
  • Easy to Use - Simple browser-based online application – just click and publish your suppliers and brands products or collections to your website in just seconds.
  • Find new suppliers - Get their product feed and grow your business.

Bottom Line: JewelExchange is a win-win solution for everyone!
