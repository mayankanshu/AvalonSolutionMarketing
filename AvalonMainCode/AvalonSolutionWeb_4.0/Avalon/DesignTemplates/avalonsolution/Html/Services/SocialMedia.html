Social Media is a platform that allows you to build and reflect social networks or relations. Social Networking Websites help people to connect and share information with each other. According to comScore's recently released report, Social Networking is the most popular online activity worldwide. Benefits of Social Networking

  • Social Networking builds your brand and promotes your business.
  • It encourages user participation and provides word of mouth publicity.
  • Social Networking allows you to communicate with your customers.
  • It generates traffic for your store and website and boosts your sales.
  • It provides you a platform to make event announcements and product launches.
  • You can upload photos and videos of your store, products and events.
  • You can promote your website on your Social Networking Profiles.
  • You can easily get customer comments and feedback for your products and services.

People use pinboards to plan their weddings, decorate their homes, and organize their favorite recipes. It is one of the fastest growing social services in the world.
